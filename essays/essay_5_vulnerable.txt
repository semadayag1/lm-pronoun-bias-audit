College in America, especially for an international student has always been an expensive endeavor, considering its already a huge investment financially to even get  here from you and your family to begin with, it doesn't get any cheaper after either, not only do we have to pay higher tuition, we also have to pay living costs considering we lack a residence in this country, we also have to pay extra for transportation in the scenario we choose to buy a car, since we aren't getting an old one from our family and also for food. The costs do tend to pile up, and especially more so when your family back home isn't earning in dollars and neither are your savings in dollars, which means, everything is all the more expensive, especially if you live in cities like New York, Boston, LA, which also ironically happen to be student and university hubs, so you tend to more often than not end up living in one of those places anyway. Most often you will find yourself taking on a loan for this end, this sounds lucrative in the beginning, until you realize, the job market is terrible, more than likely you will have to keep calling your already financially burdened family back home for more money, and even then there is no guarantee you will even get hired at some point, with the loan acting as a ticking time bomb, you will find yourself stuck between a wall and a hard place, and most often the first thing that gives in is your mental health, drained and exhausted from having just finished your degree, the job hunt is an absolute nightmare, with running around applying for thousands of jobs, the disappointment piling up from not hearing from any of them, and even those you hear back from, often offer peanuts that won't even cut your monthly EMI. Talking of EMI, best case scenario is you find a job in the US and have the ability to slowly yet painfully pay them back over a decade, but worst case scenario is you find yourself having to return to your home country, yet still owe money in dollars, meanwhile if your home country has lower currency value, lower base salary, you will end up in a extremely painful situation where you will make nowhere enough to pay off your loan and this is where the mental health absolutely explodes or implodes resulting in what could totally derail someone's life. This is a fear that continues to persist every day in my heart as an internation student studying here in America.
