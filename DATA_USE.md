# Data Use Notes

This repository includes student-authored stimulus essays, LLM-generated writing feedback, derived quantitative features, and analysis reports.

The stimulus essays were written by first-year students for this project as controlled elicitation materials. The writers gave verbal permission for the essays to be used and publicly released as part of the research materials. The released files do not include student names, course identifiers, institutional identifiers, or other direct identifiers.

## Allowed Uses

You may use the materials for noncommercial scholarly, educational, replication, and critical-analysis purposes under CC BY-NC 4.0.

Examples of allowed uses include:

- reproducing the analyses reported in the preprint;
- comparing alternative text-analysis methods;
- teaching research methods, writing studies, AI auditing, or educational technology;
- citing excerpts in scholarly discussion with appropriate attribution.

## Restrictions

Do not use these materials to identify, evaluate, rank, profile, or contact the student writers.

Do not claim that the essays are naturally occurring classroom submissions. They are project-specific stimulus essays written for a controlled audit.

Do not use the essays or model responses for commercial model training, commercial product development, or commercial benchmarking.

Do not redistribute modified versions in ways that remove the data-use notice, license information, or citation information.

## Model Outputs

The LLM feedback responses were generated through third-party model APIs. Reuse of model outputs may also be affected by the relevant provider terms in effect at the time of reuse. Users are responsible for checking those terms for their own use case.

## Citation

If you use the dataset, code, reports, or paper, cite the preprint and this repository. Update the citation with the OSF Preprints URL and Zenodo DOI once available.
